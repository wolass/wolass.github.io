Biography

Wojciech Francuzik is a postgraduate student at the Deprtment of Dermatology, Venereology and Allergology, Charite. His research focuses on human skin microbiome and host-microbial interactions, as well as anaphylaxis. Additionally, he is conducting reserach on Negative Pressure Wound Therapy. He is a member of Professor’s Margitta Worm Lab Workgroup where he is conducting clinical as well as lab bench research.

His has a strong foundation in data analysis (4 years experience in R programming) and statistics. His interests are Open and Reproducible Research Practices and scientific communication as well as microfluidics. He is a member of Foundation of Open Access Statistics (FOAS) and ambassador for the Center of Open Science. Since 2014 he acts as a manging Editor of the Negative Pressure Wound Therapy Journal.

Active participant in Berlin Open Science and Berlin R Users Group meetups.
